About SentinalX

SentinalX is a powerful upcoming cross-engine anti-cheat system. With support coming soon to Roblox, Unity, Unreal Engine, and Godot, it's designed to bring aggressive detection and synchronization into your workflow.

UISentinalX is the design interface style, inspired by Roblox Studio and modern UI trends like iOS18 and OneUI7.

More features, including AI Detection, Auto Update Sync, and Cloud Logging, are being developed.
